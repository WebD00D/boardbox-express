Have questions? We've got answers.
So how does this all work?
Every week we'll feature an upcoming boardsport brand that will be giving you an awesome deal on one of their products.
What sort of deals are we talking?
Typically you can expect to save between 10, 20, and even upwards of 50% off MSRP.
Does Boardbox ship the order?
No. When an order is placed, we send an immediate notification to our featured brand that an order has been placed and that they should pack and ship right away.
Is there free shipping?
Shipping cost is all up to the brands we feature, and where they are located. Sometimes maybe, other times their might be a shipping cost associated.
Why is there a limit on quantity I can purchase?
We're all about sharing the stoke here at Boardbox, and want to make sure everyone has a chance to get in on it.
I own a brand, how can I get featured on the Marketplace?
Awesome! We're stoked you want to be apart of the Boardbox community. Please send all inquiries to hello@getboardbox.com.

We decided to take an additional approach at accomplishing our goal of helping smaller boardsport startups get there name out there.
